Fried Rice

Description

Fried rice is a dish of cooked rice that has been stir-fried in a wok or a frying pan and is usually mixed with other ingredients such as eggs, vegetables, seafood, or meat. It is often eaten by itself or as an accompaniment to another dish.

Ingredients

  • 4 cups of rice.
  • 4 cups of sliced Cabbage.
  • 2 cups of sliced Carrot.
  • 2 cups of sliced Green beans.
  • Half cup of peas.
  • 1 cup of sliced Green pepper.
  • Cow Liver {300g}.
  • 2kg of Chicken.
  • 200ml Vegetable oil.
  • 1 cup sweet corn.
  • 1 cup of sliced Onions.
  • Curry powder (coloring spice) {2 Tablespoons}.
  • 1 teaspoon ground pepper
  • Salt to taste.

Steps

  1. Wash and precook the chicken and liver together using a teaspoon of salt, half cup of sliced onions,
    a teaspoon of thyme spice, a teaspoon of curry, two seasoning cubes teaspoon each of powdered ginger and garlic).
  2. Chop the cabbage, green beans, carrot, liver, green peppers and set aside.
  3. Parboil the 4 cups of rice also and cook with half of the meat stock (water from the meat) and two cups of water,
    more water if your rice is hard to cook. You want it almost done., about 70% done but not completely soft.
  4. Set your cooking pot on heat and add 200ml of vegetable oil. You will fry some ingredients for fried rice.
  5. Allow heating then add the sliced onions stir for 1-2 minutes.
  6. Then add the chopped carrot and green beans, any of these two can actually go first.
  7. Add the chopped liver and sweet corn, stir. And you should have your pot looking like this
  8. Add chicken stock (meat water), ground pepper (optional), a teaspoon of salt and a seasoning cube then stir and taste,
    you may add more salt.
  9. Add the green peas.
  10. Stir for 1-2 minutes then add cabbage and the curry powder for yellowish fried rice.
  11. Taste for salt, it should taste spicy and look yellowish.
  12. If it tastes nice and colorful, then add the green pepper. Stir.
  13. Combine with fried or grilled chicken and serve.
